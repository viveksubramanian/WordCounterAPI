What does this do?

This returns the text length (with and without spaces), word count and the number of times each english alphabet is repeated.

How do you use the API?

curl --header "Content-Type: application/json" \ --request POST \ --data '{"text":"hello 2 times "}' \ https://127.0.0.1:5000/analyze
